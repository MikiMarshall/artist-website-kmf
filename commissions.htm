I usually work in my studio from one or more photographs/images (these should be well lit, in focus, and include angles and details I will be asked to render). If you are local to Southport, NC, I would be happy to take these pictures myself for $20.00.

Also, I usually require a deposit of one-third the total price before I can schedule work to begin on a piece.

Prices of works in progress will not change, unless the original specifications for that work are significantly modified by you. Otherwise, the price list above may occasionally change without notice.
